We provide a wide range of services

Security Architecture Design & Review

Our team of experts can help build and review your security designs to ensure that you have adequate controls in place.

Security Risk Assessment

Our team of experts will perform a risk assessment, and work with you to help you understand the risks. We provide industry standard solutions, while keeping your unique business model in mind. It is our goal to keep any residual risks to a minimum.

Cyber Intelligence Consulting

Because our team understands security threats and their evolving landscape, we provide an industry best practice approach to the design and implementation of security controls to detect, prevent and respond to such threats.

Vulnerability Management

With an increase in cyber threats, there is a need to understand how or where your business is vulnerable. Our team will perform both internal and external vulnerability assessments to help protect you from any form of exploitation or targeted attacks.

Network Design Architecture & Review

We design, develop, and implement high-standard solutions for all your network security concerns.

Cloud Architecture

We resolve security issues for cloud vendors, both public and private. We also do this for hybrid cloud communities.

Access Control

We will create access provisions for you, based on the principle of least privilege.

Security Awareness Training

Security begins with awareness. We can educate your employees to help reduce the risks of cyber threats to your company.

Digital Forensics & Investigation

We will work with you to understand any past incidents, and create solutions to prevent the recurrence of such.

End Point Security

Having an in-depth defensive approach, we will help you understand malware behaviour, as well as other malicious actors looking to access your network. We will also help you achieve an endpoint security solution that will provide protection against both known signatures, and other behavioural anomalies.

Compliance Advisory, Security Policies & Procedure

  • Our team will work with yours to achieve all necessary risk and compliance, and regulatory requirements regarding your cybersecurity.
  • We will help you integrate technology, people, and policies.
